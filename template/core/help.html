Help & Support

How do I book a service provider?

Go to the Services page, browse by category, and click "Find" to add to your cart. Proceed to checkout to confirm.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, you can cancel any service up to 48 hours before your event date by going to your Profile > Your Events.

How do I get support?

You can reach our support team via email at 22201245@uap-bd.edu or call us at 01624253558.

Where can I view my bookings?

Log into your account and go to "My Cart" under the profile menu to see your upcoming and past bookings.
